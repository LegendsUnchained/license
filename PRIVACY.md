# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application. By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Social Authentication Data
When you choose to sign in using **Facebook** or **Discord**, we receive and store the following information provided by those platforms, based on your privacy settings:
* **Facebook:** Public profile information (name, profile picture, Facebook User ID) and email address.
* **Discord:** Your Discord username, avatar, and unique Discord ID. We may also request access to your email address associated with your Discord account for account verification.

### Device Information
To provide a personalized experience and maintain account security, we associate your account with your device. We collect:
* **Device Identifiers:** Unique hardware IDs or advertising identifiers to distinguish your specific device from others.
* **Application Metadata:** Information related to your installation of the app to facilitate synchronization and cross-device functionality.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:
* **Authenticate your identity** through secure OAuth 2.0 integration with Facebook and Discord.
* **Create and maintain your user profile** within our application.
* **Associate your user account with your specific device(s)** to ensure personalized settings, progress syncing, and secure access.
* **Improve application functionality** and provide customer support.

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

* **Service Providers:** We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers only to the extent necessary to perform tasks on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting or database management).
* **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

* **Removing Access:** You can revoke our application's access to your accounts at any time:
    * **Facebook:** Visit your [Facebook App Settings](https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications).
    * **Discord:** Visit your [Discord User Settings > Authorized Apps](https://discord.com/settings/authorized-apps).
* **Data Deletion:** You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. Please contact us at info@legendsunchained.com to initiate this process.
